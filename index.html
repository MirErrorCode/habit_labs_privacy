Privacy Policy

Habit Labs does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or non-personal user data.

All information related to habits — such as habit names, goals, timers, streaks, and relapse counts — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Habit Labs does not use analytics tools, advertising services, or third-party trackers.

Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: habitslabs.app@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
